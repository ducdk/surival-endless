Frequently Asked Questions

Is Endless Survival free to play?

Yes! Endless Survival is completely free to play in your web browser.

Do I need to create an account?

Creating an account is recommended to save your progress, but you can also play as a guest.

What browsers are supported?

The game works best on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge with WebGL support.

Can I play on mobile devices?

While the game is primarily designed for desktop, it should work on tablets and modern mobile devices.

Will there be new content added?

Yes! We're constantly working on adding new monsters, equipment, and features.

How do I report bugs?

Contact us through the support email provided in our Terms of Service page.
